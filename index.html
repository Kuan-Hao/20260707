Drone Football

Experience the futuristic aerial sport where pilots fly protective drones through an arena and score by passing a ball through the opponent's goal ring.

What is Drone Football?

Drone football is a competitive team sport using enclosed quadcopters that carry a lightweight ball. Teams work together to attack, defend, and score while avoiding collisions.

📷 Drone Football Arena Image

How to Play

  • Two teams compete.
  • One scoring drone attempts goals.
  • Defenders block opponents.
  • Highest score wins.
📷 Match Action Image

Controls

Typical controller sticks:

  • Throttle: Up/Down
  • Yaw: Turn Left/Right
  • Pitch: Forward/Back
  • Roll: Left/Right movement
📷 Controller Diagram

Flying Tips

Practice hovering, smooth turns, coordinated team movement, and obstacle avoidance before competitive play.

Repairing Drones

  1. Power off.
  2. Inspect frame.
  3. Replace damaged propellers.
  4. Check motors.
  5. Reconnect loose wires.
  6. Test safely.
